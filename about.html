Our Story

Founded to raise the bar for regional web design

VELIX Web Solutions was founded to close a gap we kept seeing: businesses across Jordan and the Gulf investing serious money in their brand, then settling for a website that undersold it. We build the opposite — premium, fast, conversion-minded websites that match the ambition of the businesses behind them.

Every project is handcrafted from the ground up: no recycled templates, no bloated builders — just clean design and code built specifically for your business and your market.
